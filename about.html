About

After serving as a Peace Corps Volunteer in the Dominican Republic, I earned my M.Ed. with a concentration in bilingual and ESL education from the University of Illinois at Chicago. Since then, I have worked as a bilingual and ESL educator, primarily in the Chicago Public Schools. I also like watercolor painting! This is my design portfolio where I experiment with website design.
